Research Experience

Research Assistant — University of Guelph / Linamar Ihub

Supervisor: Prof. Yang Sheng · Data Quality & Statistical Monitoring
May 2023 – Sept 2023

Conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Yang Sheng to improve data quality and statistical monitoring across 60 global factories. I designed an adjusted outlier detection system by integrating cycle time benchmarks with z-score intervals, enhancing anomaly detection accuracy at the machine level. Applied Linear Discriminant Analysis, independent two-sample t-tests, and other statistical methods to compare utilization between Hastech and Linergy production lines. Developed an automated alert system to update machine cycle time and provide early warnings to factory workers. Created Python-based automation scripts using SQLAlchemy, SciPy, NumPy, and Pandas to streamline data processing and reporting. This experience deepened my interest in high-dimensional data science and large-scale industrial analytics.
